# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Blog readers, developers, and students looking at technical blog posts containing visual diagrams.

## Product Purpose
A beautiful, highly interactive personal blog and documentation site powered by Astro.

## Brand Personality
Sleek, minimalist, elegant, clean typography, soft animations.

## Anti-references
Loud SaaS dashboards, overly complex grids, heavy borders, generic gray cards.

## Design Principles
- Typographic elegance
- Subtle motion
- Clear contrast and hierarchy

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance, dark/light theme support, prefers-reduced-motion styling.
